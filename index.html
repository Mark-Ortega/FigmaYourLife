About Me

My name is Bing Qi Ling! Those who know me know i am no stranger to darkness. I have face unimaginable horrors. I face evil to help lift my burdens off my shoulders. There is evil in me i can feel it... I must always keep my mind and body healthy to contain said evil. I am a "holy" monk of Fumunda, one of the top ranking monks along with my brother Xi Jinping. Together we face the horrors of evil and torment those who stand in our way. I will live and die to serve our lord Fumunda! I know three languages. I can speak the common tongue, Infernal, and Kraul. Being Trilangual can always be a benifit. I recently found a party of five and decided to journey along and so did my brother Xi. Though we are barely getting to know eachother I feel as if we might not make it far.
